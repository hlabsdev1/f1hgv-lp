FAQS

How can I upgrade my package?
You can upgrade by calling 855-342-4309 to speak to a Hilton Grand Vacations representative who will assist you.
How do I know if the package I want is still available?
A very limited number of packages are being offered at this exclusive Member price. We recommend you call 855-342-4309 as soon as possible to discuss any opportunities still available.
Can I request a special seat?
No seat assignment is required. Tickets to the HGV Clubhouse will get you access to any of the floors, where there will be elevated premium viewing opportunities and spaces with comfortable seating throughout.
Is food and beverage included?
Yes, all-inclusive food and beverage service will be available upon access to the HGV Zone.
Are children allowed to attend the event?
Children are permitted to attend with a parent or guardian. Children under 24 months do not need a ticket to enter FORMULA 1® HEINEKEN SILVER LAS VEGAS GRAND PRIX 2024.

Can I modify my reservation?
With the very limited number of packages being offered and the exclusive Member price, modifications are not permitted. However, please see the Details of Participation for our cancellation policy.
Will there be any meet and greet opportunities?
Access to the HGV Clubhouse will allow you to view the F1® cars as they weave around a 3.8-mile track comprised of three main turns and 17 corners, racing a total of 50 laps. No meet and greet events are included.
Do I have to stay at the resort?
Yes, a 3-night minimum stay is required with these packages.
Are check-in/check-out dates flexible?
In order to best accommodate all Members, each package has specific, non-changeable dates. We recommend you choose a package with check-in and check-out dates that work best for your schedule.
Do I get access to the Elara Terrace party and HGV Live! concerts during my entire stay?
Yes, all such events happening between your check-in and check-out dates are included in this package offering. Call 866-936-2293 to confirm availability.
Is there a contact for group sales?
Please call 407-488-0387 to inquire about group offerings.
Why is Hilton Grand Vacations offering these packages at such a great price?
We appreciate you being part of the Hilton Grand Vacations family and strive to bring you exceptional experiences available only to our Members and Guests. Through HGV Ultimate Access, you can take advantage of experiences like this one when you attend a membership review where you’ll learn about our latest offerings and Member benefits.
What is Hilton Grand Vacations membership review and when does it take place?
Your membership review is an approximately one-hour timeshare presentation and tour that takes place during your vacation. If you have purchased a vacation package, your membership review will be scheduled when you book your travel dates, to occur typically the day after your arrival.
Do I have to purchase ownership with Hilton Grand Vacations during my membership review?
There is absolutely no obligation to purchase an additional Hilton Grand Vacations ownership interest.
May I attend the membership review myself?
If you are married or cohabitating and combining income, you and your significant other are required to visit and attend the membership review together, except where prohibited by law.
